Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Kyrgyzstan?
The best time to visit Kyrgyzstan is from May to October when the weather is warm and mountain passes are accessible. July and August are peak season with the best weather, while May-June and September offer fewer crowds and beautiful landscapes.
Do I need a visa for Kyrgyzstan?
Citizens of most countries can enter Kyrgyzstan visa-free for up to 60 days. Please check with your local embassy for specific requirements based on your nationality.
What is the accommodation like?
Accommodation varies from comfortable hotels in cities to traditional guesthouses in villages and authentic yurt stays at Son-Kul Lake. All accommodations are clean and include basic amenities.
What fitness level is required?
This tour is suitable for anyone with moderate fitness. While some hikes are included, they are optional and can be adjusted to your ability. The most challenging part is the altitude at Son-Kul Lake (3,000m).
Can dietary requirements be accommodated?
Yes! Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when booking. We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, halal, and other dietary needs.
